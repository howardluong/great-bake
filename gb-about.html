About Us

Since the beginning, we strived for excellence utilizing our passion for baking deserts and that is what we built this business on. All of our products embrace traditional methods of baking from old school Chinese methods with an added twist of French-styled methods as well.

100% real ingredients

We use 100% natural ingredients here at The Great Bake. Our promise to deliver quality baked goods has never been broken and we plan to keep it that way. Our goal is to ensure that our products are as light and healthy as possible.

Fresh products everday

We take pride in what we do and serving you. Our handmade bakes are always prepared in store and baked on the same day. We are certain that you will taste the difference with our deserts.
